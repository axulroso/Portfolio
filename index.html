My name is Patrick Cheney, and I'm a UX/UI Designer based in San Antonio, Texas. I am a creative problem solver with 9 years of experience in education, where I taught high school marching band and concert band. My background in education has honed my skills in instructional design, enabling me to create engaging and intuitive user experiences.

My experience in the classroom has given me a unique perspective on user needs, allowing me to empathize with users and anticipate their challenges. I have a Bachelors in Education and a certification in UX/UI Design from the University of Texas at San Antonio, where I developed a strong foundation in design principles, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.
